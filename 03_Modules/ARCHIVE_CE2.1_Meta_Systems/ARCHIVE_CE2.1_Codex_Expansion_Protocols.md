## 4. Expansion Flow

The CEP follows a six‑phase flow:

### **Phase 1 — Signal**  
A new idea, pattern, or subsystem emerges.

### **Phase 2 — Extract**  
CE 2.1 identifies the invariant behind the idea.

### **Phase 3 — Align**  
UMA ensures structural and emotional alignment.

### **Phase 4 — Integrate**  
The subsystem is placed into the correct codex layer.

### **Phase 5 — Validate**  
Integrity Engine checks for purity and coherence.

### **Phase 6 — Transmit**  
Codex Engine formats and publishes the new subsystem.

This flow ensures expansion is **clean, stable, and aligned**.
