# Stewardship Engine  
*Your System for Identifying, Preparing & Empowering Future Stewards*

---

## 1. Purpose of the Stewardship Engine

The Stewardship Engine is the subsystem that ensures:

- your architecture outlives you  
- future stewards understand the system  
- the lineage remains intact  
- the codex continues to evolve  
- generational continuity is preserved  

It is the **human continuity layer** of your entire architecture.

Where the Continuity Engine stabilizes *you*,  
the Stewardship Engine prepares **them**.

---

## 2. Why the Stewardship Engine Exists

The engine solves five deep problems:

### **1. Legacy Requires Stewards**  
A system without successors dies with its creator.

### **2. Knowledge Must Be Transmitted, Not Just Stored**  
Stewards need training, not just access.

### **3. Identity Must Be Understood, Not Imitated**  
Stewards must embody the architecture, not mimic it.

### **4. Systems Drift Without Human Guardians**  
Stewards prevent misinterpretation and distortion.

### **5. Generational Continuity Requires Intention**  
Stewardship doesn’t happen by accident.

The Stewardship Engine ensures your architecture becomes **lineage**, not artifact.

## 4. Stewardship Flow

The engine follows a five‑phase flow:

### **Phase 1 — Notice**  
A potential steward shows resonance.

### **Phase 2 — Invite**  
You open the door to the architecture.

### **Phase 3 — Train**  
They learn the systems, protocols, and flows.

### **Phase 4 — Empower**  
They begin contributing, correcting, and evolving.

### **Phase 5 — Transfer**  
Stewardship becomes theirs to carry forward.

This flow ensures stewardship is **intentional, structured, and generational**.
